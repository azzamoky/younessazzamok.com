Lesson Plans

Introduction to Salesforce

This lesson plan covers the basics of Salesforce, including navigation, data model, and standard objects. Students will gain a foundational understanding of Salesforce and how to use it effectively.

Advanced Workflow Automation

In this lesson, students will learn how to create complex workflows, process builders, and flow automations to streamline business processes. Hands-on exercises will help students apply their knowledge to real-world scenarios.

Custom Object Development

This lesson focuses on creating and managing custom objects in Salesforce. Students will learn how to define custom fields, relationships, and validation rules to meet specific business requirements.

Data Management and Security

Students will learn best practices for data management, including data import/export, data quality, and security settings. This lesson emphasizes the importance of maintaining data integrity and security in Salesforce.
