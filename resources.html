Salesforce Resources

Beginner Resources

Websites

  1. Trailhead by Salesforce

    URL: trailhead.salesforce.com

    Trailhead is Salesforce's official learning platform offering interactive tutorials and projects. It's an excellent resource for beginners to learn the basics of Salesforce, earn badges, and follow learning paths tailored to different roles.

  2. Salesforce Help & Training

    URL: help.salesforce.com

    This site provides a comprehensive knowledge base and documentation for Salesforce users. It covers a wide range of topics, from getting started guides to in-depth articles on specific features.

  3. Admin Hero

    URL: adminhero.com

    Admin Hero is a blog that offers tips, tricks, and best practices for Salesforce administrators. It covers fundamental topics and provides practical advice for those just starting their Salesforce journey.

  4. SFDC99

    URL: sfdc99.com

    SFDC99 is a blog created by David Liu that helps beginners understand Salesforce development. It provides step-by-step tutorials and inspiring success stories to motivate new learners.

  5. Salesforce Ben

    URL: salesforceben.com

    Salesforce Ben is a blog that offers a wide range of articles, tutorials, and news updates about Salesforce. It's a great resource for beginners looking to stay informed and learn new tips.

  6. Automation Champion

    URL: automationchampion.com

    This blog focuses on Salesforce automation, providing guides and examples on how to use tools like Process Builder and Flow Builder. It's ideal for beginners interested in automating their Salesforce processes.

  7. Focus on Force

    URL: focusonforce.com

    Focus on Force offers study guides and practice exams for various Salesforce certifications. It's an excellent starting point for beginners preparing for their first certification.

  8. Salesforce Weekly

    URL: salesforceweek.ly

    Salesforce Weekly is a blog that covers a range of Salesforce topics, from beginner tips to advanced strategies. It's a great place to find regular updates and insights.

  9. ShellBlack

    URL: shellblack.com

    ShellBlack provides a series of video tutorials and blog posts aimed at helping beginners understand the fundamentals of Salesforce. It covers a variety of topics in an easy-to-understand format.

  10. The Wizard News

    URL: thewizardnews.com

    This blog offers tips, tricks, and tutorials for Salesforce users and administrators. It's a great resource for beginners looking to learn the basics and stay updated on new features.

Books

  1. "Salesforce For Dummies" by Tom Wong

    This book is an excellent introduction to Salesforce, offering clear explanations and practical examples. It's perfect for beginners who are new to Salesforce and want to understand the platform's core functionalities.

  2. "Teach Yourself VISUALLY Salesforce.com" by Justin Davis

    This visually oriented guide helps beginners learn Salesforce through step-by-step instructions and full-color screenshots. It's ideal for visual learners who need a straightforward introduction to the platform.

  3. "Salesforce Essentials for Administrators" by David A. Kelly

    This book provides a solid foundation for new Salesforce administrators, covering essential topics such as user management, data management, and basic automation. It's a practical guide for those starting their admin journey.

  4. "Practical Salesforce Development Without Code" by Philip Weinmeister

    Focusing on point-and-click tools, this book helps beginners understand how to develop Salesforce solutions without writing code. It's perfect for those who want to leverage Salesforce's declarative capabilities.

  5. "Getting Started with Salesforce Einstein Analytics" by Johan Yu

    This book introduces beginners to Salesforce Einstein Analytics, covering the basics of creating datasets, lenses, and dashboards. It's an excellent resource for those looking to start with analytics in Salesforce.

  6. "Salesforce Lightning Essentials" by Mohith Shrivastava

    This book provides a beginner-friendly introduction to Salesforce Lightning, covering essential topics like app development and customization. It's ideal for those new to the Lightning Experience.

  7. "Salesforce.com For Nonprofits For Dummies" by Jon Paz

    Aimed at nonprofit organizations, this book helps beginners understand how to use Salesforce to manage donors, volunteers, and other stakeholders. It's a practical guide for nonprofits new to Salesforce.

  8. "Learning Salesforce Visual Workflow" by Rakesh Gupta

    This book provides a step-by-step guide to Salesforce Visual Workflow, helping beginners automate business processes. It's a great starting point for those looking to understand Salesforce's flow capabilities.

  9. "Salesforce Reporting and Dashboards" by Johan Yu

    This book introduces beginners to Salesforce reporting and dashboards, covering basic concepts and techniques for creating effective reports. It's perfect for those looking to start with Salesforce analytics.

  10. "Salesforce.com For Dummies, Second Edition" by Tom Wong and Liz Kao

    This updated edition provides an easy-to-follow introduction to Salesforce, covering the latest features and functionalities. It's a comprehensive guide for beginners new to the platform.
